What Drives Us

365Power gym started as a yoga class. At first there were only women but as the time goes by, we saw the need to convert it into a gym. Through the commitment of three self-motivated members, 365Power gym was born.

When it started, there were few equipments and few people who were coming to the gym. In the last one year since it started, we have seen an increase in number of members. We have also added new equipments.
